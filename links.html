Useful Links

NASA Home

A website maintained by NASA dedicated to the exploration and study of exoplanets. This website serves as a hub for information and resources related to exoplanet research conducted by NASA and its partners. It provides access to a wealth of data, images, news articles, educational materials, and interactive features aimed at both scientists and the public interested in learning more about exoplanets and the search for life beyond our solar system.


NASA Exoplanet Exploration

Explore NASA's Exoplanet Exploration program and discover more about planets beyond our solar system.


NASA Eyes on Exoplanet

This interactive website allows people to visually explore the distribution of exoplanetary systems in three-dimensional space, view where the exoplanets are in our galaxy, learn what kinds of stars they orbit, and more.


Jet Propulsion Laboratory STEM Lessons:

This is a repository of educational materials and activities focused on teaching about exoplanets. Find lesson plans, activities, multimedia materials, and other resources that can be used to teach students about exoplanets and the science behind their discovery and study.
